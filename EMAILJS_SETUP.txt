# EmailJS Setup Guide
# Follow these steps to enable personalised auto-reply emails from your portfolio contact form.

## Step 1 — Create a Free EmailJS Account
1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com
2. Click "Sign Up Free"
3. Verify your email address

---

## Step 2 — Add an Email Service
1. In your EmailJS dashboard, go to "Email Services"
2. Click "Add New Service"
3. Choose your email provider (Gmail is easiest)
4. Click "Connect Account" and sign in with your Gmail
5. Click "Create Service"
6. Copy your SERVICE ID (e.g. service_abc123) — you'll need this later

---

## Step 3 — Create the Email Template
1. Go to "Email Templates" in the dashboard
2. Click "Create New Template"
3. Set the template up as follows:

   TO EMAIL field:     {{to_email}}
   FROM NAME field:    Nisarg Patel
   SUBJECT field:      Thank you for reaching out, {{to_name}}!
   REPLY TO field:     nisarg.patel@email.com  (your actual email)

   EMAIL BODY (paste exactly):
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────
   Hi {{to_name}},

   Thank you for reaching out — I really appreciate your interest.

   Your message has been received successfully, and I'll personally review it shortly. You can expect a response from me within 24 hours.

   If your matter is urgent, feel free to follow up directly via LinkedIn.

   Looking forward to connecting.

   Warm regards,
   Nisarg Patel
   Software Engineer | Cloud Enthusiast
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────

4. Click "Save"
5. Copy your TEMPLATE ID (e.g. template_xyz789) — you'll need this later

---

## Step 4 — Get Your Public Key
1. Go to "Account" → "General"
2. Copy your PUBLIC KEY (e.g. aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoP)

---

## Step 5 — Update Contact.jsx
Open src/components/Contact.jsx and replace the three placeholder values at the top:

   const EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID  = "YOUR_SERVICE_ID";   ← replace with your Service ID
   const EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID = "YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID";  ← replace with your Template ID
   const EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY  = "YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY";   ← replace with your Public Key

Example after filling in:
   const EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID  = "service_abc123";
   const EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID = "template_xyz789";
   const EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY  = "aBcDeFgHiJkLmNoP";

---

## Step 6 — Install EmailJS & Run
In your terminal:
   npm install
   npm start

---

## How It Works
When someone submits the form:
1. EmailJS sends the auto-reply to the visitor's email ({{to_name}} is replaced with their actual name)
2. You also receive a notification at your connected Gmail
3. The form shows a success screen confirming the email was sent

## Free Plan Limits
- 200 emails per month
- 2 email templates
- No credit card required

This is more than enough for a personal portfolio.
